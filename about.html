About ERM & Associates Advisory

ERM & Associates Advisory is a premier strategic and regulatory advisory firm specializing in the intersection of finance, economics, and advanced mathematics.

Founded and led by Mr. Eduardo Royo Mas—an accomplished professional with extensive experience at UBS in investment fund management and a strong academic foundation in mathematics and economics from the University of London—ERM & Associates Advisory delivers high-level counsel to clients seeking sophisticated solutions in complex regulatory and strategic matters.

Our firm operates at the forefront of analytical rigor and strategic insight, bridging quantitative expertise with practical financial industry knowledge. Mr. Royo Mas’s unique background empowers the firm to navigate regulatory landscapes, optimize strategic outcomes, and drive impactful results for elite clientele.

ERM & Associates Advisory is committed to excellence, confidentiality, and influence—providing clients with an unparalleled advantage in today's competitive and evolving markets.
